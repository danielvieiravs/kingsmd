About Us

With over a decade of affiliation through numerous automotive industries, Jason King found fulfilment from the polished touch of shine to mirror finishes. This passion of his led to the creation of JK Movements & Detailing.

At JK Movements & Detailing we are dedicated to bringing your vehicle back to cosmetic pristine condition and sustaining a strong level of customer service. We act like an extension of your own team, taking something that can be time-consuming, complicated and expensive, and turning it into a simple one-off instruction. We believe it's all about knowing what you want and providing the service that your vehicle needs.

Our team of specialised and dedicated technicians have provided Mobile Automotive Detailing and Vehicle Movement Services to a host of dealerships and manufacturers across the UK as well as individual customer requirements all over Europe. It is JK Movements & Detailing's mission to bring out the near new in every old, maintain the new to keep it looking as good as gold while focusing on an ultimate experience for our customers.
